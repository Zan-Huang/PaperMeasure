The Milton Academy Sorting Hat

About this Project:

Created by Charlotte Moremen, Alex Rodriguez, and Zan Huang, this project uses machine learning to match your writing with your peers at both Milton Academy publications. If you paste in a sample article you have written into the text area below, the sorting hat will assign you to the Milton publication which your writing style matches most. The machine learning analyzes a combination of grammar and vocabulary to find the best fit. The algorithm can currently assign writers to these publications with 86% accuracy. Best of luck in the sorting ceremony!
